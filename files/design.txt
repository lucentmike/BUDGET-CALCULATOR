Title: Budget-Calculator
Description: An interactive program that takes in your monthly income. Seperates your expenses into Needs (50), Wants (30), and Savings/Debt (20). 
             Using the 50/30/20 Rule, shows you want each category should have, and what your actual result is based on your inputs. Then generates
             a Pdf file with the Name, Month, Expected Budget, and Actual Budget       
Objects:
        User
            user_name 
            income
            needs(name, amount)
            wants(name, amount)
            savings(name, amount)
        Budget
            period
            expected_budget(user)
            actual_budget(user)
        PdfReport
            filename
            generate(user_name, budget)
